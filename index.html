WHO WAS AVICII

Tim Bergling, better known as Avicii, wasn’t just a producer, he was the heartbeat of a generation. Through his music, he gave a voice to emotions we couldn’t describe, blending electronic rhythm with human warmth. His songs like Wake Me Up, The Nights, and Waiting for Love became anthems of hope, reminding us to live fully, dream boldly, and never stop moving forward.

Behind the name Avicii stood a humble artist, a dreamer, and a visionary who changed the landscape of electronic music forever. His sound crossed borders, his melodies united strangers, and his light continues to shine through the beats that still echo on every dance floor.

Even though he’s gone, the world still dances to his music, because some lights never fade.

AVICII 4EVER

AVICII FOREVER: THE TRIBUTE was born from the desire to keep his spirit alive, not only through music, but through shared emotion. This is more than a festival; it’s a space for connection, memory, and light.

Every stage, every visual, every sound is designed to honor his story, to bring fans from all around the world together in the same heartbeat that once defined an era. Here, the past and present merge in a celebration of everything Avicii created: unity, joy, and love through sound.

We gather not just to listen, but to feel again. To remember the nights that changed us, and to dance once more, for him, and for the music that will live forever.

WHERE IT ALL COMES TOGEHTER

The heart of AVICII FOREVER: THE TRIBUTE beats in Stockholm, Sweden, the city where Tim Bergling was born, and where his journey as Avicii first began.

The festival takes place in Gärdet Park, a wide open space surrounded by nature, light, and sky, the perfect setting to reconnect with the energy his music gave us.

This is more than a venue. It’s a return home. A place where fans from every part of the world gather to celebrate his life, his sound, and his message.

Beneath the northern lights and summer skies, we dance again, not just to remember him, but to continue what he started. Because the world may have changed, but the feeling he created will never fade.

DJ PERFORMANCES

The heartbeat of AVICII FOREVER: THE TRIBUTE comes alive through the artists who once stood beside him, friends, collaborators, and pioneers who helped shape the sound of a generation.

This year’s line-up brings together some of the biggest names in electronic music: David Guetta, Nicky Romero, Otto Knows, John Dahlbäck, Shermanology, NERVO, and Martin Garrix, DJs who not only shared the stage with Avicii, but also shared his vision of unity through music.

Each of them brings their own light to the tribute: new remixes, live sets, and emotional performances inspired by the songs they created together, from I Could Be the One to You’re Gonna Love Again and beyond.

Together, they’ll transform Stockholm into a living memory of what Avicii started, a rhythm that still connects millions around the world.
